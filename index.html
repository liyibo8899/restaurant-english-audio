1. Calling Austin Public Health About Food Permits

致电 Austin 公共卫生部门咨询食品许可

Goal: Ask what a fixed restaurant needs before opening.
目标：确认固定餐厅开业前需要准备哪些卫生许可和检查。

Owner
Good morning, I am opening a dumpling restaurant in Austin.

早上好，我准备在奥斯丁开一家饺子餐厅。

Officer
Great. For a fixed food establishment, you need to submit the application and schedule inspections.

好的。固定食品经营场所需要提交申请并安排检查。

Owner
Could you tell me which documents I should prepare first?

您能告诉我应该先准备哪些文件吗？

Officer
Please prepare your floor plan, menu, equipment list, business information, and contact information.

请准备平面图、菜单、设备清单、企业信息和联系方式。

2. Asking About Sales Tax Permit

咨询德州销售税许可

Goal: Register before selling taxable meals.
目标：在销售应税餐食前完成销售税许可登记。

Owner
I need to apply for a Texas sales tax permit for my restaurant.

我需要为餐厅申请德州销售税许可。

Agent
You can apply online through the Texas Comptroller eSystems portal.

您可以通过德州主计长 eSystems 门户在线申请。

Owner
How long does it usually take to receive the permit?

通常多久可以收到许可？

Agent
Please allow two to three weeks after you submit a complete application.

提交完整申请后，请预留两到三周时间。

3. Calling a Commercial Real Estate Broker

联系商业地产中介

Goal: Describe the restaurant concept and location needs.
目标：说明餐厅定位和选址需求。

Owner
I am looking for a restaurant space for dumplings and Chinese dishes.

我正在找一个卖饺子和中餐的餐厅铺位。

Broker
What size, budget, and neighborhood are you targeting?

您目标的面积、预算和社区位置是什么？

Owner
I prefer fifteen hundred to two thousand square feet near offices or apartments.

我希望是一千五百到两千平方英尺，靠近办公楼或公寓。

Broker
I will send you available listings with restaurant infrastructure.

我会发给您带餐饮基础设施的可用房源。

4. Touring a Restaurant Space

实地看餐厅铺位

Goal: Check kitchen, hood, grease trap, utilities, and parking.
目标：检查厨房、排烟罩、隔油池、水电气和停车条件。

Owner
Does this space already have a commercial hood and grease trap?

这个铺位已经有商用排烟罩和隔油池吗？

Broker
Yes, the previous tenant operated a small restaurant here.

有的，上一任租户在这里经营过一家小餐厅。

Owner
Can we verify the gas capacity and the condition of the walk-in cooler?

我们能核实燃气容量和步入式冷库的状况吗？

Broker
I will ask the landlord for maintenance records and utility details.

我会向房东索要维修记录和公用设施详情。

5. Negotiating a Letter of Intent

谈判租赁意向书

Goal: Discuss rent, term, free rent, and tenant improvement allowance.
目标：沟通租金、租期、免租期和装修补贴。

Owner
Before we sign the lease, I would like to discuss the letter of intent.

签租约之前，我想先讨论租赁意向书。

Landlord
What terms are most important for your restaurant?

哪些条款对您的餐厅最重要？

Owner
I need a reasonable base rent, three months of free rent, and a tenant improvement allowance.

我需要合理的基础租金、三个月免租期和租户装修补贴。

Landlord
Send me your proposal, and we can review it this week.

把您的提案发给我，我们本周可以审核。

6. Talking With an Architect or Permit Consultant

与建筑师或许可顾问沟通

Goal: Confirm plan review and construction permit path.
目标：确认图纸审查和施工许可路径。

Owner
We need drawings for a dumpling kitchen, dining room, and restroom updates.

我们需要饺子厨房、用餐区和洗手间更新的图纸。

Consultant
I will check zoning, occupancy, plumbing, electrical, and mechanical requirements.

我会检查分区、用途、水管、电气和机械系统要求。

Owner
Please tell me which permits must be approved before construction starts.

请告诉我施工开始前必须批准哪些许可。

Consultant
I will prepare a permit matrix and a submission timeline.

我会准备许可矩阵和提交时间表。

7. Coordinating With a Contractor

与装修承包商沟通

Goal: Control scope, timeline, change orders, and inspections.
目标：控制施工范围、时间表、变更单和检查。

Owner
Please give me a written estimate with labor, materials, and schedule.

请给我一份包含人工、材料和工期的书面报价。

Contractor
I can provide a detailed estimate after we inspect the site.

我们检查现场后，我可以提供详细报价。

Owner
Any change order must be approved by me in writing before work begins.

任何变更单都必须在开工前由我书面批准。

Contractor
Understood. I will document every change and inspection milestone.

明白。我会记录每项变更和检查节点。

8. Preparing for Health Inspection

准备卫生检查

Goal: Show readiness: temperatures, handwashing, storage, sanitizer.
目标：展示准备情况：温度、洗手、储存、消毒液。

Inspector
Can you show me your handwashing sinks and sanitizer test strips?

可以给我看洗手池和消毒液测试纸吗？

Owner
Yes, they are stocked, labeled, and easy for staff to reach.

可以，它们已经备齐、贴好标签，员工也容易拿到。

Inspector
How do you monitor refrigerator and hot holding temperatures?

你们如何监控冰箱和热保温温度？

Owner
We record temperatures every two hours on this log sheet.

我们每两小时在这张记录表上记录温度。

9. Interviewing a Server

面试服务员

Goal: Evaluate availability, service attitude, and food safety awareness.
目标：评估排班时间、服务态度和食品安全意识。

Owner
Tell me about your restaurant experience and availability.

请介绍一下您的餐厅经验和可上班时间。

Applicant
I have two years of serving experience and I can work evenings and weekends.

我有两年服务经验，可以上晚班和周末班。

Owner
How would you handle a guest who says the order is wrong?

如果客人说订单错了，您会怎么处理？

Applicant
I would apologize, confirm the correct item, and ask the kitchen to remake it quickly.

我会道歉，确认正确菜品，并请厨房尽快重做。

10. Training Front-of-House Staff

培训前厅员工

Goal: Standardize greetings, order taking, allergy questions, and table checks.
目标：统一迎宾、点单、过敏询问和巡台标准。

Owner
Please greet every guest within ten seconds and make eye contact.

请在十秒内问候每位客人，并保持眼神交流。

Staff
Should we mention the soup dumplings and lunch specials?

我们需要介绍小笼汤包和午餐特价吗？

Owner
Yes. Also ask about allergies before sending the order to the kitchen.

需要。把订单送到厨房前，也要询问过敏情况。

Staff
I will repeat the order to confirm everything is correct.

我会复述订单，确认所有内容正确。

11. Greeting and Seating Guests

迎接并安排客人入座

Goal: Welcome customers and manage wait time.
目标：欢迎客人并管理等位时间。

Host
Welcome in. How many people are in your party today?

欢迎光临。今天您一共有几位？

Guest
We have four people, and we would like a table by the window.

我们四位，想要靠窗的桌子。

Host
I can seat you by the window in about ten minutes.

大约十分钟后我可以安排您坐靠窗位置。

Guest
That works. Please put our name on the waitlist.

可以。请把我们的名字加入等位名单。

12. Handling a Customer Complaint

处理顾客投诉

Goal: Apologize, fix the issue, and protect the guest relationship.
目标：道歉、解决问题并维护顾客关系。

Guest
Excuse me, these dumplings are cold in the middle.

不好意思，这些饺子中间是冷的。

Server
I am very sorry. Let me replace them right away.

非常抱歉。我马上为您更换。

Guest
Thank you. We are in a hurry.

谢谢。我们赶时间。

Server
I understand. I will ask the kitchen to prioritize your order.

我理解。我会请厨房优先处理您的订单。
